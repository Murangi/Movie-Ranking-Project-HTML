My top 3 Anime of all time

Death Note

"Death Note" is a popular anime series that follows the story of Light Yagami,a high school student
who discovers a mysterious notebook with the power to kill anyone whose name is written in it. As
Light takes on the alias "Kira" and embarks on a mission to eliminate criminals and create a utopia,
he attracts the attention of L, a brilliant detective determined to stop him. The series delves into
themes of morality, justice, and the corrupting influence of power, featuring intense psychological
battles and a complex cat-and-mouse game between Light and L.

Hunter X Hunter

"Hunter x Hunter" is an acclaimed anime series that follows the adventures of Gon Freecss, a young
boy who discovers that his long-lost father is a legendary Hunter, a professional who specializes in
finding rare treasures, exploring uncharted territories, and hunting dangerous individuals. Determined
to follow in his father's footsteps, Gon takes the grueling Hunter Examination and befriends other
aspiring Hunters, including the vengeful Kurapika, the aspiring doctor Leorio, and the mysterious
assassin Killua. The series is renowned for its complex characters, intricate plotlines, and exploration
of themes such as friendship, ambition, and the nature of humanity.

Run with the Wind

"Run with the Wind" (Kaze ga Tsuyoku Fuiteiru) is an inspiring sports anime that centers on Kakeru
Kurahara, a former elite runner who finds himself without direction until he meets Haiji Kiyose, a
university student with a dream of assembling a competitive long-distance running team. Haiji recruits
Kakeru and eight other residents of their dormitory to compete in the prestigious Hakone Ekiden relay
marathon. Despite their varied backgrounds and initial lack of experience, the team members bond over
their shared struggles and determination. The series beautifully portrays their growth, teamwork, and
perseverance, highlighting the transformative power of pursuing a common goal.
